    Traveling is an activity which helps us to know our world in a better way. It fills us with enthusiasm to explore new places and cultures of different parts of the world. It opens doors of opportunities to learn and understand different cultures, beliefs and lifestyles of people living in the world. Traveling not only broadens our understanding and knowledge but also helps us to gain experience and memories which can be cherished for the rest of our lives.Local traveling involves visiting places which are situated in the same region or nearby. This type of traveling often includes visiting nearby tourist attractions such as mountains, lakes, religious places, museums, gardens, and other historical places. Local traveling is a great way to explore nearby places and learn more about the culture and lifestyle of people living in the same region.
